ABOUT US

Our Background

De’ Plaisant Café was established to produce the combination of local traditional dessert, “kuih” with the western elements. The words of “De’ Plaisant” are derived from French with the amalgamation of “De” means of and “Plaisant” means pleasant. Both words represent the way on how we want to produce a high quality menu, specifically desserts that give pleasure and satisfaction to our customers. This cafe specializes in producing and selling desserts, coffee and smoothies with affordable prices ranging from RM 5 till RM 15.

Vision

Create beautiful memory through our delightful dessert innovation to meet the tastes of users.


Mission

Serving fusion unique desserts with satisfying dining experience at affordable price.
